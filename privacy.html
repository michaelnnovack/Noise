Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Our noise detection app processes audio data locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to your microphone recordings.

Microphone Access

The app requires microphone access to measure ambient noise levels. Audio data is processed in real-time and is never saved or transmitted.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand app usage and improve user experience. This includes anonymized usage statistics and performance metrics.

Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. These ads may use cookies to provide relevant content based on your browsing behavior.

Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact us through our website.
